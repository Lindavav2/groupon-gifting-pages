📧 Recipient didn't receive email/SMS with voucher 709 cases · 10% resolved ▸
Buyer sent a gift voucher but the recipient didn't receive it via email or SMS. Root causes: email blocked by spam filters, wrong address entered at send time, SMS not delivered, or recipient looks in My Groupons instead of their inbox.

Our response: Look for an email from notify@r.groupon.com → click "Add to My Groupon". Alternatively use SMS or QR code from a printed voucher. ⚠️ UX gap: recipient doesn't know where to look — email is not an obvious entry point for a new user ⚠️ Gift delivery relies entirely on email deliverability with no fallback

🎁 Buyer can't find the "Give as a gift" button 263 cases · 7% resolved ▸
Buyer sees "go to My Groupons and click Give as a gift" in the confirmation email — but can't find the button in the UI. Three root causes: (1) the deal is not giftable, (2) the button doesn't appear immediately — there is a ~10-minute delay after purchase, (3) UI labels differ between desktop ("Give as a gift") and mobile app ("Regift").

Our response: My Groupons → Give as a gift (desktop) or Regift (app). If the button is missing, wait 10 minutes. If the deal isn't giftable, options are limited. ⚠️ UX gap: email instructions and actual UI labels don't match (desktop vs. mobile) ⚠️ 10-minute delay is not communicated to the customer — appears as a bug

🔴 Gift voucher shows "already redeemed" 255 cases · 3% resolved ▸
Recipient's gift voucher shows as used even though the recipient never redeemed it. Three root causes: (1) Groupon "1 voucher per customer" limit — merchant refuses a second voucher for the same business even when it belongs to the gift recipient, (2) technical error during transfer to recipient's account, (3) merchant marks voucher as redeemed simply by scanning the QR code to view it.

Our response: Escalation — bot cannot resolve this. Lowest resolve rate of all categories: 3%. 🔴 Critical gap: 97% of these cases go unresolved 🔴 "1 per customer" limit penalises gift recipients who unknowingly violate a rule they were never shown

👁️ Gift voucher not visible in recipient's account 134 cases · 12% resolved ▸
Recipient accepted the gift voucher (or believes they did) but it doesn't appear in My Groupons / My Stuff. Most common cause: recipient is logged in with a different email than the one the voucher was sent to, or the "Add to account" link in the notification email didn't complete correctly.

Our response: Look for an email from notify@r.groupon.com → Add to Groupon Account. Confirm you're logged in with the correct email. ⚠️ Recipient onboarding gap: gift recipient arrives on Groupon without context and without guidance ✅ Relatively resolvable — if the recipient can find the right email
